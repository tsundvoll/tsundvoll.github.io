The Sourdough Bread Factory

What is a sourdough bread?

A sourdough bread is a bread where the dough of the bread is based on a sourdough starter instead of using regular yeast. You can read more about sourdough here.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Sourdough starter
  • Salt

Recipe

  1. Mix flour, water and sourdough starter in a bowl
  2. After 1 hour, mix in salt
  3. Knead the dough gently for 5-10 minutes
  4. Let the dough rest for 20-30 minutes, then stretch and fold the dough from all directions
  5. Repeat the step above 2 more times
  6. Let the dough rest (Bulk rise) for 2-4 hours
  7. Pour the dough onto a flat, clean surface
  8. Divide the dough into 3 equal sized doughs and tighten them up
  9. Let the doughs rest for 25-30 minutes, tighten them up again and have them into baking forms
  10. Place the forms in the fridge over night (8-12 hours)
  11. Sprinkle some rice flour over the breads and score them with a knife
  12. Bake at 250° with a pan of water beneath for 20 minutes
  13. Remove the pan of water and bake at 225° for 20-25 minutes
  14. Let the breads cool on a rack before slicing
